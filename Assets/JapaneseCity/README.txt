
--------------------
Japanese City Tokyo
--------------------


Description:
--------------------
This package contains 3d assets to construct your Japanese City Tokyo.

Content
--------------------
- Buildings
- Japanese Sign Boards
- Street Module set
- Street Props

- 2 Demo scenes
- 196 FBX
- 186 Prefabs
- 2 Textures (Type:Albedo map and Emissive Map / Size:2048x2048 )
- And 2 Alternative resolution textures (Size:4096x4096)

Triangle Count
----------------------
Building: 50 - 2500
Street module asset: 30 - 550
Street Prop: 10-110

Info
--------------------------
*Buildings has no interior.
*This package contains only 3d art, no scripts or musics.
*All screenshots and videos are captured on PC build.
